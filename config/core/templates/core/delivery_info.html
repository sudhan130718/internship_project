Delivery Information

Delivery Timeline

We process and dispatch all wholesale toy orders within 2-3 business days. Delivery to rural areas may take 5-7 business days depending on your location.

Shipping Policy

  • We offer delivery to most rural areas across Tamil Nadu and nearby states.
  • Orders above ₹5,000 are eligible for free delivery.
  • Orders below ₹5,000 incur a flat delivery fee of ₹150.

Special Note for Rural Shops

We understand the challenges of rural logistics. Our delivery partners are trained to ensure smooth doorstep delivery even in remote regions. If your area is hard to reach, we will contact you in advance to coordinate delivery.
