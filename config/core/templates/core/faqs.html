Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Do you deliver to remote rural areas?

Yes! We specialize in supplying toys to rural shops. If your area is hard to reach, we will coordinate with local delivery partners to ensure your order reaches you safely.

2. How much is the delivery charge?

Delivery is free for orders above ₹5,000. Orders below that amount carry a flat ₹150 delivery charge.

3. How long will delivery take?

Orders are dispatched within 2-3 business days. Rural deliveries may take 5–7 business days depending on your location.

4. Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, a tracking ID will be sent to your email or phone number. You can also track it from your account dashboard.

5. What if I receive damaged toys?

We ensure careful packaging, but if any product arrives damaged, contact us within 48 hours with a photo. We’ll arrange a replacement or refund based on the issue.
